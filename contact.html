Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start care services?

For standard services, we can typically begin within 24–48 hours after the initial assessment. For emergency situations, we can often arrange care within 2–4 hours. The exact timeline depends on your specific needs and caregiver availability.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Greater Johannesburg area including Sandton, Randburg, Rosebank, Bryanston, and surrounding suburbs. For specialised services, we may be able to arrange care in other areas—please contact us to discuss your location.

How are your caregivers vetted?

All caregivers undergo rigorous screening including criminal background checks, reference verification, qualification validation, health screening, and comprehensive interviews. They also receive ongoing training and regular performance evaluations.

Can I meet the caregiver before services begin?

Yes, we encourage a meet-and-greet session before services begin. This allows you to get to know the caregiver, discuss specific needs, and ensure it's a good match. There's no charge for this introductory meeting.

What happens if my regular caregiver is unavailable?

We maintain a team of backup caregivers who are familiar with our standards and procedures. If your regular caregiver is unavailable, we will provide a suitable replacement and ensure they are fully briefed on your care plan.
