Our Care Categories

At NorthServices Care, we offer a full spectrum of home care services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Each service is tailored to provide maximum comfort, safety, and dignity.

Elderly & Companion Care

Our core service focuses on maintaining the dignity and independence of seniors in their own homes. We provide essential daily assistance and the compassionate companionship that is so crucial for mental well-being.

Ideal For: Seniors who require non-medical support, socialization, and general assistance with daily routines while maintaining independence at home.

Services Include:

  • Meal preparation and feeding assistance
  • Light housekeeping and laundry
  • Running errands and grocery shopping
  • Medication reminders (non-administration)
  • Mobility and transfer assistance
  • Companionship and mental stimulation
  • Personal hygiene and grooming
  • Transportation to appointments

Home Nursing & Clinical Support

For clients with more complex needs, our registered nurses and clinical team provide professional medical care at home. This service helps manage chronic illnesses, post-operative recovery, and complex care plans.

Ideal For: Post-surgical recovery, chronic illness management, palliative care, and long-term medical needs requiring professional support.

Services Include:

  • Wound care and dressing changes
  • Vitals monitoring (blood pressure, glucose, etc.)
  • Medication management and compliance support
  • Collaborating with doctors and specialists
  • Support with rehabilitation exercises
  • Palliative and end-of-life support alongside family
  • Education for family members on home care
  • Care documentation and reporting

Disability & Special Needs Support

We provide structured, respectful support for individuals with physical or intellectual disabilities, including children. Our plans are highly individualised, focusing on empowering the client and assisting their family.

Ideal For: Clients with disabilities or special needs requiring assistance with personal care, community participation, behavioural support, or family respite.

Services Include:

  • Personal hygiene and grooming assistance
  • Support with school, work or community activities
  • Therapy support routines (as directed by therapists)
  • Respite care and support for family caregivers
  • Mobility and positioning support
  • Support with communication and social skills
  • Encouraging hobbies, play and mental stimulation
  • Structured routines to reduce anxiety and stress
